How to Test in Editor

  1. Open the video editor: index.html?id=6
  2. Click on any video clip in the timeline
  3. The Video Edit Modal will open
  4. Look at the top - you'll see the Clip Timeline Preview
  5. Adjust the Begin or Duration values
  6. Watch the timeline update in real-time

Visual Indicators

  • Gray Background: Total video duration
  • Purple Gradient: Selected clip segment (begin to begin+duration)
  • White Label: Shows exact time range (e.g., "5.0s - 10.0s")
  • Markers: Show 0s and total video duration
  • Hover Effect: Segment scales slightly on hover
